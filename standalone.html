What is a standalone and what is it for?

It is a program that, in our case, loads the Transformice game outside of a browser, independently, as the name says (translated), the features are to give you more configuration options, shortcuts, fullscreen and alignment, to make certain things in the game easier, it is not necessarily an advantage in the game, its functions can replace, for example, the use of a larger monitor or avoid lag using shortcuts.


This standalone is created in flash, and needs a suitable program to get it working. You can open the standalone on your PC and it runs normally (so you already have this program installed on your machine). If your standalone gives an error when opening it, just install the Adobe Air on your machine and you will be able to play normally.
